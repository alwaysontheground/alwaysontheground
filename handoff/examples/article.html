Documentary · 52 min · 2024

The Sound of
Broken Glass.

Beirut · 33.8938°N 35.5018°E · recorded Feb—Nov 2023

Four years after the explosion that tore open the port of Beirut, one street in Mar Mikhael is still learning what the neighbourhood sounds like now.

We filmed over eight months with three families who live on Armenia Street. A glazier who no longer sleeps. A kindergarten teacher who has learned to teach in a half-light. A brother and sister who were five and seven on 4 August 2020 and who are now, quietly, ten and twelve.

The film has no narrator. It moves at the speed of its street. It was made slowly, on purpose.
